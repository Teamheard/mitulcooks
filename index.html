The Making of Aurora Baldanialis

This unique cocktail was concocted at 1 AM in Sooke, BC, inspired by the mesmerizing dance of the northern lights. The Aurora Baldanialis captures the essence of that unforgettable night, blending the unconventional with a touch of the spectacular.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz gin
  • 150 ml lukewarm tap water
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Served in a previously used dirty wine glass

Instructions
